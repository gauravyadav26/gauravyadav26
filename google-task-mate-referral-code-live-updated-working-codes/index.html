Google Task Mate Referral Code ( Live Updated Working Codes )

November 27, 2020November 27, 2020 Gaurav Yadav

Google Task Mate App has been recently released in India, which allows users to earn money by doing simple tasks on their smartphones.

You might be aware of the Google Opinion Rewards, but it has limited options to earn. Google Task Mate provides better options to earn and the biggest plus point is that you can transfer the money to your online wallet.

Installing Google Task Mate

  • Download Task Mate app from Google play store.
  • When you open the app, it’ll as to login with your Gmail account.
  • After, Select your preferred language (English, Hindi, Tamil etc) and Click on continue
  • Enter the Referral code which we have provided some working invitation Codes below
  • Accept the terms and conditions and proceed next (Please note that you will be proceeded to the next section of taskmate App only if you enter valid referral invitation code or else you may get error)
  • Once your Invitation Code is successfully validated, You will get to see the available tasks in TaskMate App.
  • Complete the tasks accurately and submit them. Google will review and verify them. You can check all approved and pending tasks under ” My Tasks Sections”
  • When you’re ready to cash out, simply register your e-wallet or account with Google payment partner in the app
  • Visit your profile page and hit the “cash out” button. You can then withdraw your earnings in your local currency.
